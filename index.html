About me

Hello, my name is Ned. I like to hike, camp, SCUBA dive, see live music and attend festivals, and travel. So basically all the cliche hobbies out there. My favorite food is steak, though I am trying to give it up and move towards a more plant based diet. But old habits die hard. I'm eager for a career change and excited to see just how deep the developer rabbit hole goes. I have lived in Los Angeles for eight years and love what this city has to offer; it has a little bit for everyone. I am an N-64 junkie (Mario Party for life). You never realize how little you have to say about yourself until you have to write it out. Slave to the rave, see you on the floor.
